ABOUT US

Green Horizon was built by a passionate team of students, innovators, and earth advocates who share one goal: to fight climate change through creativity and technology. 🌿

We believe that real change starts with small, consistent actions. By combining gamified learning and daily eco-reminders, we empower people of all ages to make sustainable choices in their everyday lives.



Our mission is not just to inform — but to inspire. We envision a world where protecting the planet becomes second nature. Every click, choice, and challenge brings us closer to a brighter, greener tomorrow.

How It Works



Play Interactive Games

Explore fun and educational games that teach you about conservation and sustainability.

Complete Eco-Challenges

Why Choose Green Horizon?

Because learning about climate change doesn’t have to be serious or boring. Green Horizon makes it interactive, rewarding, and fun. We bring together education, technology, and motivation to help people live sustainably — one mission at a time.

Whether you’re just starting your eco-journey or already leading green initiatives, this platform helps you stay inspired and connected with others who share the same purpose.
