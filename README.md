# Green Horizon – Community-Driven Environmental Engagement Platform

## Overview

**Green Horizon** is a web-based community platform designed to encourage environmental awareness and sustainable living through interactive activities, social engagement, and gamification. The project aligns with the **United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)**, particularly those focused on climate action, sustainable communities, responsible consumption, and environmental protection.

The platform transforms environmental responsibility into an engaging experience by allowing users to participate in daily eco-friendly challenges, share their contributions with the community, and earn rewards through a comprehensive points system.

---

## Objectives

* Promote environmental awareness and sustainable habits.
* Encourage community participation in environmental initiatives.
* Educate users about environmental issues through interactive quizzes.
* Motivate positive behavior using gamification and reward mechanisms.
* Build a supportive online community dedicated to protecting the environment.

---

## Key Features

### 🌿 Daily Environmental Challenges

Users can complete daily eco-friendly tasks such as:

* Reducing plastic usage
* Conserving water and electricity
* Planting trees or plants
* Proper waste segregation
* Participating in local clean-up activities

Successful completion of challenges rewards users with points.

### 📸 Community Posts

Users can:

* Upload photos as proof of completed challenges.
* Add captions describing their environmental actions.
* Share experiences and inspire others within the community.

### ❤️ Social Interaction

The platform includes social networking features where users can:

* Like posts from other members.
* Engage with community-generated content.
* Encourage and motivate fellow environmental advocates.

### 🧠 Environmental Quizzes

Interactive quizzes help users:

* Learn about environmental issues and sustainability.
* Test their knowledge on ecological topics.
* Earn additional points for correct answers.

### 🏆 Gamified Reward System

A point-based system increases user engagement by rewarding:

* Completion of daily challenges.
* Participation in quizzes.
* Active contribution to the community.

Accumulated points can be used for:

* User rankings
* Achievement badges
* Leaderboards
* Recognition within the Green Horizon community

### 📊 User Progress Tracking

Users can monitor:

* Completed challenges
* Earned points
* Quiz performance
* Community contributions
* Environmental impact achievements
